About

BD Economics is a project by Brian Dew to provide useful and timely information on both recent macroeconomic trends and techniques for working with economic data.

Brian Dew ( @bd_econ)

I'm an economic researcher focused on the U.S. labor market and international trade. I've worked previously with the Center for Economic and Policy Research, the Statistics Department of the International Monetary Fund, and in the Capital and Emerging Markets Department of the Institute of International Finance. I hold a master's degree in economics from American University in Washington, DC and a bachelor's degree in pyschology and organizational sciences from the George Washington University.
