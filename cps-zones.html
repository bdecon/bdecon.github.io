What are the 70 zones?

These 70 zones are a custom geography, not an official Census or BLS classification. Every CPS respondent is assigned to one zone so the survey's ~60,000 monthly households pool into stable sub-national areas big enough to measure. They come in four kinds (below); click any zone to find it on the map.
