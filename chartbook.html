About the Chartbook

The US Economic Chartbook is an open-source reference for analyzing the US economy. It bridges the gap between raw data resources like FRED and professional analysis like the IMF Article IV—more curated than databases, fresher than traditional analysis.

Contributions and corrections are welcome via GitHub.
